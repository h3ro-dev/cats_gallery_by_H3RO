CERT Mint & Stake Tutorial

This tutorial provides formal, step‐by‐step instructions for minting and staking a CERT on the Chrysalis platform.

Text Walkthrough

Step 1: Prerequisites

Before purchasing a CERT, ensure you possess the necessary tokens. Chrysalis operates on Polygon's pre Layer 1, requiring you to hold POL (Polygon's native token for transaction fees) and USDT for the direct purchase.

Step 2: Accessing the Decentralized Application (dApp)

If you encounter a website blocked error, it indicates that Chrysalis is not selling these products in your country. To view the project, you must use a VPN to route your IP address to an approved region.

Step 3: Dashboard Overview

Upon accessing the dApp, the dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of your account activity and notifications.

Step 4: Wallet Connection and Verification

To proceed with your CERT purchase, follow these authentication steps:

  1. Email Authentication: Click the profile icon (indicated by a red dot) to access the email authentication form.
  2. Terms Confirmation: Review and confirm the displayed terms and conditions.
  3. Wallet Connection: Select your preferred digital wallet from the available options.
  4. Wallet Verification: Verify your wallet using the six-digit code sent to your email. Please check your spam folder if necessary.

Step 5: CERT Purchase Process

After successful authentication and wallet connection, proceed to the CERT purchase interface. Here, review the available tiers and associated metrics:

  • Efficiency: Indicates the harvesting capability of the CERT, expressed as a percentage. A higher percentage denotes greater token harvesting performance.
  • Multiple: Determines the replication factor during phase two, where your initial CERT may be multiplied by a specified rate.

Once you have made your selection, click the "Checkout Now" button to complete your order.

Step 6: Staking Your CERT

Following the purchase, navigate to the Stake Tokens page to deposit your CERTs and begin earning rewards.
